MANAGED SECURITY SERVICES.

" A typical mid-sized business in Africa will have at least one or two systems exposed to the internet with little or no security to detect or prevent an attack. Such systems will have default passwords creating vulnerabilities that internal technology or ICT support are not aware of."

- Africa Cyber Security Report 2016

The Serianu Managed Security Services (SMSS) provides locally focused, comprehensive and robust security services that incorporate highly skilled security experts and industry proven processes that seamlessly integrate with your existing infrastructure.

SMSS is a viable alternative to traditional security technology delivering a world-class suite of security solutions that are nonvendor specific to ensure that each client gets the solutions best suited to their particular situation.

GOVERNANCE & STRATEGY.

Ensure information security and risk management strategy is mapped to the organisation's strategic business objectives.

REMEDIATION SERVICES.

Ensure effective and efficient remediation of process and control gaps including policies, procedures, breach, training and awareness, technical control implementation and improvement of business processes.

LOG MANAGEMENT & MONITORING.

Establish continuous 24/7 event log monitoring, real-time response to threats, alerts, patches and updates.

SECURE NETWORK & INFRASTRUCTURE.

Implement and maintain secure and resilient information architecture and services.

VULNERABILITY & THREAT MANAGEMENT.

Identify, classify, remediate and mitigate vulnerabilities within the network.

DISASTER RECOVERY & CONTINUITY.

Build business resilience to ensure continuity through strategic initiatives for backup, recovery,archive, disaster recovery and business continuity.

IDENTITY & ACCESS MANAGEMENT.

Reduce identity and access violations by enhancing the creation, deactivation, deletion of accounts, user provisioning and access rights.
