ACIC

The Africa Cyber Immersion centre (ACIC) is a state-of-the-art research, innovation and training facility that seeks to address Africa's ongoing and long-term future needs through unique education, training, research, and practical applications.

OUR MISSON.

Our mission is to engage, educate and empower private and public organisations through research, innovation and training.

OUR VISION.

The Africa Cyber Immersion Centre will be recognized as a world-class cyber security research, innovation and training facility focused on reducing the cyber security risk to African organisations through real-world practice, education, public-private collaboration and thought leadership.

Long term goals and strategy.

EDUCATION AND TRAINING.

To provide education, training, and research infrastructure to enhance public and private sectors in cybersecurity preparedness, while building a world-class cyber security professionals

RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT.

To enhance research and development opportunities through leading edge technology. To provide local organisations with Provide small businesses with the tools to grow and compete by allowing access to technology and education resources through the Africa Cyber Immersion Centre.

CYBERSECURITY AND PROTECTION.

To Increase protection and security of public resources and provide efficient and effective response to cyber related emergencies.
