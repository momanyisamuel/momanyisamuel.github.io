CONSULTING SERVICES.

We help our customers collect, protect, and analyze critical business information. Serianu specializes in providing cutting edge consulting services around new and emerging technology areas including information security, data analytics and technology advisory – network security, application security, cloud security and cyber security.

CYBERTHREAT VULNERABILITY MANAGEMENT.

This service enables organisations to design and build architectures that integrate cyber intelligence and analytics to proactively identify threats and risks while enabling rapid incident response capabilities resulting in improved operational support enhancing business requirements.

INSIDER THREAT MITIGATION AND IDENTITY ASSURANCE SERVICE.

The Serianu Insider Threat & Identity Assurance is a first-of-its-kind, integrated service for unparalleled visibility of insider threats activity with intelligent threat detection and prevention framework.

LOG MANANAGEMENT SERVICE.

The Serianu Log Management Service provides a robust log collection, alerting and archival solution that is designed to be an important component of your organization’s risk management strategy, providing you with valuable tools to help your organization address its applicable compliance requirements.

INFORMATION RISK AND COMPLIANCE MANAGEMENT.

The Serianu Risk Management approach leverages standards such as ISO 27002, COSO, ITIL, CoBIT and other IT security best practices; we work with you to establish a customized framework that best fits your specific compliance and security requirements.
