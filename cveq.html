CVEQ™

The Cyber Visibility and Exposure Framework (CVEQ™) is an innovative risk quantification approach that enables organisations to measure and quantify their cyber security risk.

Components.

Inherent Risk Profile

Maturity and Benchmarking Assessment

Cyber Visibility Statement

Cyber Exposure Analysis

Risk Reduction Cost Analysis

Key Goals.

Determine Cyber visibility posture of an institution through metrics and Quantification.

Determine Exposure value for an institution and Risk reduction cost.

Executive Reporting: Visibility Statements (Cyber Security Balance Sheet and Income Statements).
